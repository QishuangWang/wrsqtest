    Company profile

    无人商圈是一家致力于为广大用户提供便捷购物生活的自助购物平台。我公司目前已有上千家入驻商户，平台上架商品总资产累计上亿。通过不断优化购物体验，精选优质加盟商，我公司力争达到国内自助购物平台第一家。
     
    Business Circle is a self-service shopping platform dedicated to providing convenient shopping for the vast number of users. At present, there are thousands of merchants in our company, and the total assets of the goods on the platform are up to hundreds of millions. Through continuous optimization of shopping experience and selection of high quality franchisees, our company strives to reach the first domestic self-help shopping platform.
